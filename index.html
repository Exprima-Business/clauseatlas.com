The Problem

Public sector compliance is a maze. Nobody's built the map — until now.

Government contractors face a web of overlapping regulations that no commercial compliance tool was designed to handle.

Regulations everywhere, no single source

FAR, DFARS, NIST, OPM, Executive Orders, agency-specific rules — requirements are scattered across dozens of sources with no unified view.

Cross-references that never end

One DFARS clause references a NIST standard, which maps to an Executive Order, which triggers an OPM requirement. The chain is almost impossible to follow manually.

Commercial tools don't speak government

Existing platforms cover SOC 2, ISO, and HIPAA. But when your contract has a DFARS 252.204-7012 clause or an OPM privacy requirement, you're on your own.
