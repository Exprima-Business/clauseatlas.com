About ClauseAtlas

We're building the compliance platform that the public sector has always needed — but never had.

The Problem

Businesses working with the federal government face a maze of regulations — FAR clauses, DFARS requirements, NIST frameworks, OPM directives, Executive Orders, and more. These rules are scattered across hundreds of documents, written in dense legal language, and constantly changing.

Existing compliance platforms were built for commercial frameworks like SOC 2, ISO 27001, and HIPAA. They don't understand the unique complexity of public sector regulations — the cross-references between clause families, the flow-down requirements, or the nuanced implementation guidance that government contracts demand.

Our Mission

ClauseAtlas was built from the ground up to solve this problem. We believe that proving compliance with government regulations shouldn't require a team of consultants, months of manual work, and stacks of spreadsheets.

Our platform scans your documents, maps them against 186 clauses across 28 regulation families, and gives you a clear picture of your compliance posture — in minutes, not months.

Built by Exprima

ClauseAtlas is developed by Exprima, a team with deep experience in government technology, compliance, and software engineering. We've lived the pain of navigating public sector regulations firsthand, and we built ClauseAtlas to be the tool we always wished existed.

What Sets Us Apart

  • The only compliance platform focused exclusively on public sector regulations
  • Coverage across FAR, DFARS, NIST, OPM, Executive Orders, and more
  • Intelligent document scanning that understands regulatory language
  • Cross-reference mapping between related clause families
  • Built for the way government compliance actually works
